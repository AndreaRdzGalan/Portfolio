About me

Since I was little I love the cartoons, and drawing it and color them. I also love taking photos of animals, plants or products. And the idea of merging the photo with illustration arose thanks to a school project where we had to create a story but incorporate textures of real objects through photographs. In the same class we made a comic using only photographs, so I made mine with Harry Potter stickers. And I enojoyed thhose projects so much that I wanted to continue expanding that technique into my illustration, as it combines my two favourite hobbies unto one.
